Part O and D2 were printed on a Creality CR-10s 

Using Ziro PLA (http://ziro3d.com/pla-filament/)

The part was designed in TinkerCAD (https://www.tinkercad.com/)

It was printed at a 1.2 Layer height with a 20% Gyroid infill, No supports, 200/50 for the nozzle and print bed Temprture. 

Save Files included for both parts are .OBJ, .STL, .glb and .SVG
